Program Description

The Global Excellence Award is Schola's flagship scholarship initiative, aimed at nurturing the next generation of world-class leaders, researchers, and innovators. We believe that financial barriers should never stand in the way of academic excellence.

Recipients are selected based on their intellectual curiosity, leadership potential, and a proven track record of community service. Whether you are pioneering a new technology or advocating for social justice, this scholarship provides the financial foundation for you to thrive.

Requirements

  • Minimum GPA of 3.8 on a 4.0 scale
  • Enrolled in a full-time undergraduate program
  • Two letters of academic recommendation
  • 1,000-word personal statement

Benefits

  • Full tuition coverage for one academic year
  • Rp3000.000 monthly living stipend
  • Mentorship from industry professionals
  • Access to global alumni networking events
