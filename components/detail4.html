Program Description

The Global Ethics Scholarship is a specialized fellowship curated by Schola to foster advanced philosophical, sociological, and ethical analysis among modern student researchers. This program targets humanities scholars entering intense field-driven research or cross-border cultural data projects.

Through curated structural modules with humanities panels across the United Kingdom, chosen students gain more than financial backing. Recipients enter targeted discussion pools, receiving active publication assistance, access to historic resource archives, and immediate integration into international ethical councils.

Requirements

  • Enrolled in Philosophy, Sociology, or related Humanities programs
  • Minimum Cumulative GPA of 3.40 or equivalent
  • A completed 500-word essay on a current ethical dilemma
  • One recommendation letter from a humanities faculty member

Benefits

  • Direct stipend payout of Rp20.000.000
  • Subsidized access to digital humanities archives and libraries
  • Invitation to the annual UK-Schola Ethics Symposium
  • Mentorship tracks covering publishing and thesis development

Application Process

  1. Online Profile & Document Submission
  2. Ethics Essay Blind Evaluation
  3. Virtual Panel Review Chat
  4. Final Stipend Allocation Notice
