Program Description

The Entrepreneurial Spirit Fund is Schola's flagship corporate commerce grant, established alongside leading venture pools in Australia. This program focuses entirely on underwriting undergraduate business students who integrate commercial models with corporate governance or social accountability goals.

In addition to alleviating partial baseline academic overheads, selected fellows are automatically integrated into local startup labs. Recipients receive seed network connection resources, workshop coaching on capital matching, and direct desk workspace allocations in modern corporate hubs.

Requirements

  • Enrolled full-time in a corporate commerce or business administration track
  • Minimum Cumulative GPA of 3.45 on a 4.0 scale
  • A cohesive 3-page executive summary for a sustainable business project
  • One recommendation letter from an economics faculty mentor

Benefits

  • Partial tuition funding support allocation
  • Fully funded entry to leading commercial workshops in Melbourne
  • Direct strategic coaching sessions with corporate founders
  • Priority tracking for post-graduation workspace incubator funding

Application Process

  1. Business Profile & Portfolio Submission
  2. Executive Strategy Paper Assessment
  3. Interactive Interactive Pitch Interview
  4. Final Allocation Selection Results
