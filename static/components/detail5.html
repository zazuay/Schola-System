Program Description

The Digital Arts Visionary Grant is a highly progressive studio incubator fund managed via Schola. It provides production support packages to innovative minds building installations, virtual environments, creative coding assets, interactive animations, or high-concept digital exhibits based in France.

Beyond foundational project capital injections, selected artists enter specialized production residences across cultural tech hubs. Recipients gain dedicated gallery showcase spaces, cloud rendering server allocations, and direct networking access to contemporary European design directors.

Requirements

  • Enrolled or admitted to a digital media or creative arts undergraduate track
  • Minimum Cumulative GPA of 3.30 or portfolio equivalent
  • A digital portfolio showcasing active original projects or concepts
  • A short video presentation detailing the proposed project's vision

Benefits

  • One-time direct financial grant of Rp30.000.000
  • Subsidized licensing for professional creative software suites
  • Feature spotlight in Schola's international design catalog
  • Coaching on intellectual property and media distribution networks

Application Process

  1. Online Profile & Portfolio Submission
  2. Creative & Technical Evaluation
  3. Virtual Pitch & Concept Interview
  4. Final Funding Notice & Onboarding
