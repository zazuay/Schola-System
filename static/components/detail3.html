Program Description

The Maple Leaf Academic Fund is an elite financial gateway designed to gather prominent student figures across international communities. This foundation heavily rewards individual character, non-profit track records, organizational management capabilities, and high academic milestones.

Fellows selected into the Maple Leaf track undergo an enriched cross-cultural system, incorporating regional development bootcamps and structural mentorship directly run by elite alumni bodies based across Canada's key corporate and governmental sectors.

Requirements

  • Minimum Cumulative GPA of 3.50 or equivalent
  • Proven leadership roles in community projects / student bodies
  • Two non-profit or institutional reference letters
  • A detailed 750-word community impact roadmap essay

Benefits

  • 100% Full Tuition exemption coverage
  • Annual health insurance and university administration fees covered
  • Stipend support package for academic resource materials
  • Access to leadership development programs in Ottawa & Toronto

Application Process

  1. Administrative Portfolio Submission
  2. Community Roadmap Pitch Evaluation
  3. Interactive Board Interview Block
  4. Final Selection Allocation Result
