Program Description

The International Excellence Grant stands as a premier funding infrastructure within Schola's ecosystem, designed specifically to break international boundaries for premium student talents. This fund focuses heavily on cross-cultural academic integration and supports students entering advanced research tracks.

Through partnership networks across leading global institutes, recipients do not merely receive economic backing but are automatically funneled into institutional mentoring pipelines. Ideal candidates are those possessing clear long-term milestones that align with regional development and community transformations.

Requirements

  • Non-German citizenship status
  • Minimum Cumulative GPA of 3.75
  • Certified language proficiency score (IELTS/TOEFL)
  • Research proposal or academic intent essay

Benefits

  • Partial tuition funding up to Rp75.000.000
  • Annual seminar allowance packages
  • Direct integration into European alumni networks
  • Immigration and visa administration guidance

Application Process

  1. Online Profile Submission
  2. Academic Portfolios Evaluation
  3. Panel Panel Interview Block
  4. Grant Allocation Notice
